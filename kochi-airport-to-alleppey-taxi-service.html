Seamless Kochi Airport to Alleppey Taxi Service

Written by Anand | June 28, 2026

Transition directly from your flight straight to your relaxing houseboat cruise. Our Kochi Airport to Alleppey taxi service provides a smooth, scenic drive down the coast so your backwater holiday begins the moment you land.

Distance and Route Timing

Alleppey (Alappuzha) is located approximately 85 kilometers from Cochin International Airport. The drive down via National Highway 66 takes about 2 to 2.5 hours, depending on regional traffic conditions around Ernakulam bypass routes.

Direct Houseboat Jetty Drop-Off

Navigating the narrow paths to the specific houseboat boarding points (like Punnamada Finishing Point or Pallathuruthy jetty) can be highly confusing for out-of-town drivers. Our local chauffeurs know the backwaters intimately and will drop you right at your specific boarding jetty, safely assisting with your luggage blocks.

Relaxed Return Transfers

After a completely relaxing overnight stay on the backwaters, the last thing you want is a stressful, rushed ride back to catch a flight. We closely track your departure time and schedule your return pickup from Alleppey with perfect buffer time, ensuring a relaxed end to your holiday.
