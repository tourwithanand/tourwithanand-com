Book Your Sabarimala Taxi from Kochi Airport to Pamba

Written by Anand | June 28, 2026

Planning your holy pilgrimage to Sabarimala? Booking a direct, private taxi from Kochi Airport (COK) to the Pamba base camp ensures your group travels safely and arrives rested to begin the traditional trek.

Route and Real Travel Time

The journey from Kochi Airport to Pamba covers roughly 150 to 160 kilometers and usually takes around 4.5 to 5 hours. The drive passes through Perumbavoor, Muvattupuzha, Thodupuzha, and Erumeli before ascending the forest roads.

Navigating the Pilgrim Season Safely

During the busy Mandalam-Makaravilakku season, traffic on the route to Pamba is incredibly heavy, and driving at night through mountain fog requires expert skill. Our chauffeurs have years of experience handling the Sabarimala routes, ensuring a safe, calm journey no matter your arrival hour.

Important Ritual Stops for Devotees

We deeply respect the traditions of the pilgrimage. Our drivers will happily coordinate stops at the Erumeli Sree Dharma Sastha Temple for the traditional Petta Thullal, and ensure your group has ample time to refresh before reaching the Pamba parking grounds.
