About Cochin International Airport (COK)

Located in Nedumbassery, approximately 25 kilometers northeast of the city center, Cochin International Airport is the busiest and largest airport in the state of Kerala. As the world’s first fully solar-powered airport, it features sprawling, highly active terminals (Terminal 1 for Domestic and Terminal 3 for International flights). Because the airport is situated on the outskirts of the city, relying on random on-demand transportation can result in long wait times, unexpected surge pricing, or drivers unwilling to undertake long-distance intercity trips to the mountains or backwaters.

By pre-booking a private airport transfer Kochi, you bypass the crowded taxi queues and the unreliability of standard app-based cabs.
