About Anand

Founder & Curator of Bespoke South Indian Journeys

A Personal Welcome

Hi dear guest, this is Anand from Kochi, Kerala, the beautiful Indian state aptly called “God’s own country”. My professional team and I are on a journey to provide the very best of our lovely state to our beloved clients from across the world.

My journey into the tourism industry began in 2009 after a successful career with BMW in Muscat. The one poignant fact that struck me early on was a serious dearth of trained, language-proficient professionals and drivers capable of catering to the high standards expected by international and national guests.

As the years progressed, I realized that a huge chunk of service productivity was being eroded by middlemen. They were siphoning off much higher rates compared to actual service providers, rendering the tourists a disservice. I strongly felt this breach of trust should be mended. Hence, I decided to take the plunge and connect with our clients directly, ensuring better rates and superior service.

Our Work Philosophy

We specialize in taxi operations, tour packages, and travel experiences across South India. Our team is compact, educated, and well-trained, with a strong impetus on honesty, discipline, punctuality, and cleanliness.

It is my pleasure to speak with each client personally. Before finalizing any itinerary, we take the time to understand your needs. Only when your requirements match our level of service do we move forward. No false promises, no hidden charges, and no loss of trust—this is my solemn promise to you.

I am immensely grateful that you have spent your valuable time reading about our philosophy. I look forward to hearing from you. Have a lovely day!

Warmest regards,
Anand
