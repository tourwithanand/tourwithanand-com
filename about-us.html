About Us

CRAFTING EXCEPTIONAL JOURNEYS ACROSS GOD'S OWN COUNTRY

A Personal Welcome from Anand

Hi dear guest, this is Anand from Kochi, Kerala, the beautiful Indian state aptly called “God’s own country”. My professional team and I are on a journey to provide the very best of our lovely state to our beloved clients from across the world.

My journey into the tourism industry began in 2009 after a successful career with BMW in Muscat. The one poignant fact that struck me early on was a serious dearth of trained, language-proficient professionals and drivers capable of catering to the high standards expected by international and national guests.

Our Work Philosophy

As the years progressed, we analysed that a huge chunk of service productivity was being eroded by middlemen. We strongly felt this breach of trust should be mended by us, the actual service providers. Hence we decided to take the plunge and connect with our clients directly.

We specialize in taxi operations, tour packages and other related activities for experiencing entire South India. Our team is compact, educated and well trained, with a strong impetus on honesty, discipline, punctuality and cleanliness.

It’s our pleasure to talk and deal with each and every client so that before finalising the travel itinerary, both you and we will have the opportunity to understand each other. No false promises, hidden charges or loss of trust—this is my solemn promise.

Warmest regards,
Anand
Founder, Tour With Anand
