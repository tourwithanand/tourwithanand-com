Frequently Asked Questions

Important information for international and domestic travelers arriving in Kochi.

1. How do I book a taxi for late-night flight arrivals?

We operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Simply pre-book your ride via WhatsApp, and your chauffeur will be waiting for you regardless of your landing time.

2. Do you charge extra if my flight is delayed?

No. We actively track your flight status in real-time. If your flight is delayed, we adjust the pickup time automatically without any hidden waiting charges.

3. How will I find my driver at the airport?

We offer a Meet & Greet service. Your driver will be waiting near the arrival gate exit holding a personalized placard with your name on it.

4. Are your drivers fluent in English?

Yes, our professional chauffeurs speak clear English, ensuring smooth communication for our guests from Europe, the Middle East, and Southeast Asia.

5. Are there any hidden tolls or parking fees?

We believe in 100% transparent pricing. The quote we provide includes all airport parking fees, road tolls, and driver bata. You will never face surprise charges.

6. Can I book a taxi from the airport directly to Munnar?

Absolutely. Airport-to-Munnar is one of our most popular routes. Our drivers are highly experienced in navigating the hill station roads safely, even at night.

7. Do you provide multi-day tour packages from the airport?

Yes! We can pick you up from the airport and serve as your dedicated transport for your entire 5-day or 7-day Kerala itinerary.

8. How far in advance should I book my airport taxi?

While we can accommodate last-minute requests, we highly recommend booking at least 24 to 48 hours in advance to guarantee your preferred vehicle type.
