Tempo Traveller from Kochi Airport to Pamba: Group Travel Guide

Written by Anand | June 28, 2026

When traveling to Sabarimala with a large group of devotees, a Tempo Traveller is the most economical, unified, and comfortable choice. Learn everything you need to know about navigating from Kochi Airport to Pamba in a luxury coach configurations.

Dedicated Space for Your Group and Irumudikettu

A standard taxi simply cannot accommodate the unique storage needs of a large pilgrim group. Our premium **Tempo Travellers** provide isolated, spacious rear luggage areas for your heavy luggage bags and sacred Irumudikettu, ensuring the main seating cabin remains completely uncluttered and respectful.

12-Seater and 17-Seater Premium Options

Depending on the size of your Swamy group, you can choose between our highly comfortable 12-seater executive models or our larger 17-seater coaches. All configurations come equipped with high-back push seats, powerful multi-zone air conditioning, and pristine interior design layouts to ensure relaxation during the 5-hour drive.

The Most Cost-Effective Way to Travel

Booking a single Tempo Traveller is vastly more affordable than hiring three or four separate cars. It also guarantees that your entire group travels together, takes synchronized temple and food breaks at Erumeli, and arrives at the Pamba base camp at the exact same moment to begin the holy climb.
