Kochi to Munnar Premium Taxi: Travel the Hills in Comfort

Written by Anand | June 28, 2026

The winding 130-kilometer drive from Kochi Airport to Munnar requires an experienced driver and a vehicle with excellent suspension. Discover why our premium Innova and Ertiga taxis are the safest way to ascend the beautiful tea hills.

Distance and Travel Time Breakdown

The distance from Cochin International Airport (COK) to Munnar is approximately 110 to 120 kilometers. Depending on traffic, season, and mist conditions, the ascent into the hills takes about 3.5 to 4 hours.

Why Choose a Premium Taxi for the Hill Descent?

The road to Munnar is filled with sharp hairpin bends and steep inclines. Traveling in a premium vehicle like a Toyota Innova Crysta ensures you don't feel the road friction. The dual air-conditioning and superior suspension heavily reduce the chances of motion sickness, making it the safest choice for elderly travelers and young children.

Scenic Stops Along the Way

Your transfer isn't just a taxi ride; it's the start of your holiday. Our drivers know exactly where to pull over for the best local experiences:

  • Cheeyappara & Valara Waterfalls: Perfect, crisp spots to stretch your legs and take photos.
  • Spice Plantations: Take a quick guided walk to see how Kerala's famous cardamom and pepper are grown naturally.
