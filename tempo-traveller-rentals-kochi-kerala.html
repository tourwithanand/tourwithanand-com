Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking a Tempo Traveller in Kochi.

1. How many passengers can your Tempo Travellers accommodate?

We offer multiple configurations ranging from 12-seater, 14-seater, and 17-seater standard models, up to ultra-luxury Force Urbania and 26-seater mini coaches.

2. Do you provide pickup directly from Kochi Airport (COK)?

Yes, we operate 24/7. Your chauffeur will be waiting at the arrival terminal with a placard, regardless of whether your flight lands during the day or late at night.

3. Is there enough space for luggage?

Absolutely. Our premium Tempo Travellers come with dedicated rear luggage space as well as secure roof carriers to easily accommodate heavy suitcases and bags.

4. Are the vehicles air-conditioned?

Yes, all our Tempo Travellers feature powerful, well-maintained dual AC systems with individual vents for passenger comfort.

5. Can we hire a vehicle for outstation trips?

Yes. We carry valid interstate permits, allowing seamless group travel from Kochi to outstation destinations like Ooty, Kodaikanal, Madurai, and Coorg.

6. Are your drivers experienced in hill station driving?

Safety is our top priority. Our chauffeurs have years of accident-free experience navigating the challenging, winding roads of Munnar and Wayanad.

7. Are there any hidden charges in your pricing?

No. We believe in complete transparency. Our quotes typically include fuel, driver bata, tolls, and parking, so you know the exact final cost upfront.

8. Do you organize Sabarimala pilgrimage trips?

Yes, we offer specialized itineraries for Sabarimala and other major South Indian temple tours, ensuring comfortable transit for elderly passengers and large families.
